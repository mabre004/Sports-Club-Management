ABOUT OUR CLUB

Abu Dhabi Sporting Club is home to 23 sporting facilities and stadiums. We offer a wide array of both team and individual sports, including football, tennis, squash, and swimming.

In addition to our facilities, we have qualified coaches who are ready to assist you in any sport you'd like to learn regardless of your level!

Opening hours: everyday from 9am to 12pm.

Address: Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi

For inquiries: Send us a message and we'll get back to you!
